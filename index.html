CLOUD OF NEWS - WORLD HEADLINES

Why are we here?
The proposal of this website is to gather worldwide headlines and display them in a wordcloud format, so you can get a glimpse of what is going on around the world.


What is a wordcloud?
According to Google it is an image composed of words used in a particular text or subject, in which the size of each word indicates its frequency or importance.
